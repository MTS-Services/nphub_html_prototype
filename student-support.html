❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
You can reset your password by clicking the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your inbox. If you don't receive the email within 10 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.
How do I apply for clinical rotations?
Navigate to the "Applications" page from your dashboard. Browse available rotations, review requirements, and click "Apply" on rotations that match your program needs. Make sure your profile is complete and all required documents are uploaded before applying.
How do I log my clinical hours?
Use the "Log Hours" page to record your daily clinical activities. Enter the date, hours worked, activities performed, and any notes. Your preceptor will need to approve your logged hours before they count toward your program requirements.
What if my preceptor hasn't responded to my messages?
If your preceptor hasn't responded within 48 hours, try calling their office directly. If you still can't reach them, contact our support team and we'll help facilitate communication or find alternative solutions.
How do I upgrade to Pro?
Click the "Upgrade to Pro" button in the header or visit your Profile page. Pro membership gives you unlimited rotation applications, priority support, advanced search filters, and detailed analytics on your progress.
Who do I contact for billing questions?
For billing inquiries, email billing@npclinicalhub.com or call (555) 987-6543. You can also view your payment history and manage your subscription from the "Payment History" page.
Can I change my rotation after it's been approved?
Changes to approved rotations require coordination between you, your preceptor, and your program coordinator. Contact support immediately if you need to make changes, as this may affect your graduation timeline.
How do I update my profile information?
Go to the "Profile" page from the sidebar menu. You can update your personal information, academic details, upload compliance documents, and manage your emergency contacts. Keep your profile current to ensure smooth rotation placements.
